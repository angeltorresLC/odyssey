Score 🔊
/skɔːr/

To get points in a game.
Ex 1: He scored a goal yesterday!
Ex 2: What is the score of the match?
Match 🔊
/mætʃ/
⚽
A sports game between two teams.
Ex 1: The soccer match starts at 5 PM.
Ex 2: This is the most exciting match!
Throw 🔊
/θrəʊ/
⚾
To send something through the air with your arm.
Ex 1: She threw the ball very far.
Ex 2: Throw the ball to me!
Catch 🔊
/kætʃ/
🧤

Ex 1: The goalkeeper caught the ball.
Ex 2: Can you catch this?
Win 🔊
/wɪn/

⛳ A golf ball is ____ than a basketball.

🏃 A marathon is ____ than a 100m sprint.

🦘 Type the missing letters: She jumps high____ than the other players.

🐢 Type the missing word: A turtle is ____ than a rabbit. (slow)

💪 Unscramble: Type the word for "R N S G O T E R" (More strong).

✅ True or False: "Faster" is a comparative word.

❌ True or False: A race car is slower than a bicycle.

✅ True or False: We use "the" before a superlative, like "the fastest".

❌ True or False: "Tallest" is a comparative form.
