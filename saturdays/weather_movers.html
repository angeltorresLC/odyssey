📚 Weather Vocabulary Guide

Review these words before you launch your balloons!

☀️ Sunny
When the sun is shining brightly.
It is a beautiful sunny day.
☁️ Cloudy
When the sky is full of clouds.
It is cloudy, I think it will rain.
🌧️ Rainy
When water falls from the sky.
I need my umbrella because it is rainy.
💨 Windy
When the air is blowing fast.
It is very windy, let's fly our kite!
❄️ Snowy
Cold, white flakes falling in winter.
The children are making a snowman.
⛈️ Storm
Very bad weather with thunder and lightning.
We must stay inside during the storm.
🌡️ Temperature
How hot or cold the air feels.
The temperature is very high today.
🌈 Rainbow
A colorful arch in the sky after the rain.
Look at the red and yellow in that rainbow!
☂️ Umbrella
You hold this to protect yourself from rain.
Don't forget your blue umbrella!
🧥 Coat
A thick jacket you wear to stay warm.
Put on your coat, it is freezing!
💧 Puddle
A small pool of water on the ground.
Peppa loves jumping in muddy puddles.
