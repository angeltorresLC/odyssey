Review the target vocabulary and grammar forms before launch.

🥶 Freezing
Extremely cold; turning to ice.
It's freezing outside, isn't it?
🌫️ Fog
Thick cloud close to the ground.
It was hard to see the road in the fog.
⛈️ Thunderstorm
A heavy storm with thunder and lightning.
The thunderstorm which hit us was loud.
🌧️ Pouring
Raining very heavily.
It started pouring while we were walking.
🌡️ Temperature
Measurement of how hot or cold it is.
The temperature is dropping quickly.
⚡ Lightning
A flash of bright electrical light in the sky.
Look at that bright flash of lightning!
🌤️ Clear up
When clouds go away and the sky is sunny.
I hope the weather will clear up soon.
📺 Forecast
A prediction of future weather.
The forecast says it will rain tomorrow.
