👩‍⚕️ HEALTH VOCABULARY - FLYERS A2 👨‍⚕️

Ambulance 🔊
🚑
A special van that takes you to the hospital quickly.
Ex 1: Call an ambulance quickly! He is hurt!
Ex 2: The white ambulance drove fast to the hospital.
Dentist 🔊
🦷
A doctor who looks after your teeth.
Ex 1: I have to go to the dentist today because my tooth hurts.
Ex 2: The dentist told me to brush my teeth every morning.
Pharmacy 🔊
🏪
A shop where you buy medicine.
Ex 1: We need to get some pills from the pharmacy.
Ex 2: My mum went to the pharmacy to buy some medicine.
Plaster 🔊
🩹
A small sticky tape you put on a cut.
Ex 1: Put a plaster on your finger so it stops bleeding.
Ex 2: I need a pink plaster for the small cut on my knee.
Cut 🔊
✂️
A small hole in your skin made by something sharp.
Ex 1: I have a small cut on my hand from the paper.
Ex 2: Wash the cut with warm water so it gets clean.
Break 🔊
🦴
To hurt a bone so it is in two pieces.
Ex 1: Don't fall out of the tree or you will break your arm!
Ex 2: Be careful on the ice, you don't want to break your leg.
Burn 🔊
🔥
A hurt on your skin from something very hot.
Ex 1: Don't touch the hot cooker, you will get a bad burn.
Ex 2: The hot soup gave me a small burn on my tongue.
Ill 🔊
🤢
Feeling bad, not healthy or sick.
Ex 1: She didn't go to school because she felt ill.
Ex 2: When I feel ill, I usually sleep in my bed all day.
Fever 🔊
🌡️
When your body is too hot because you are sick.
Ex 1: He has a high fever and needs to drink water.
Ex 2: You have a fever, so your face feels very hot.
Fall over 🔊
🏃‍♂️
To drop to the ground.
Ex 1: He was running very fast and he fell over.
Ex 2: Be careful on the wet floor or you will fall over.
Healthy 🔊
🍎
Having a good, strong body.
Ex 1: Eating lots of fruit and playing sports makes you healthy.
Ex 2: You should drink a lot of water to stay healthy.
Unhealthy 🔊
🍔
Bad for your body.
Ex 1: Eating too much sugar and fast food is very unhealthy.
Ex 2: Sitting on the sofa all day without moving is unhealthy.
Bandage 🔊
🤕
A long piece of white cloth you wrap around an injury.
Ex 1: The kind nurse wrapped a bandage around my arm.
Ex 2: He has a bandage on his head because he fell down.
X-ray 🔊
🩻
A special picture that shows the bones inside your body.
Ex 1: The doctor took an X-ray to see if my arm was broken.
Ex 2: The X-ray showed that his leg bone was fine.
Patient 🔊
🛌
A person who is sick and going to the doctor or hospital.
Ex 1: The doctor smiled and talked to the sick patient.
Ex 2: There are many patients waiting at the hospital today.
Medicine 🔊
💊
Something you take to feel better when you are ill.
Ex 1: I have to drink this nasty medicine twice a day.
Ex 2: The doctor gave me some medicine for my bad cough.
