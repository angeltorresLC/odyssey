Beautiful 🔊
/ˈbjuː.tɪ.fəl/
🌸
Very pretty to look at.
Ex 1: This painting is beautiful.
Ex 2: Look at the beautiful bird.
Happy 🔊
/ˈhæp.i/
😃
Feeling good and smiling.
Ex 1: The dog is very happy.
Ex 2: She is a happy girl.
Drawing 🔊
/ˈdrɔː.ɪŋ/
🖍️
Making a picture with a pen right now.
Ex 1: She is drawing a cat.
Ex 2: I am drawing my house.
Playing 🔊
/ˈpleɪ.ɪŋ/
🎮
Doing a fun game right now.
Ex 1: He is playing tennis.
Ex 2: They are playing with the dog.
Mine 🔊
/maɪn/
🎒
Belonging to me.
Ex 1: That blue bag is mine.
Ex 2: The small puppy is mine.
Yours 🔊
/jɔːz/
🎁
Belonging to you.
Ex 1: Is this red book yours?
Ex 2: The happy cat is yours.
Hers 🔊
/hɜːz/
🎀
Belonging to a girl or woman.
Ex 1: The beautiful painting is hers.
Ex 2: The jacket is hers.
Above 🔊
/əˈbʌv/
☁️
Higher than something.
Ex 1: The bird is flying above the tree.
Ex 2: The picture is above the desk.
Below 🔊
/bɪˈləʊ/
🐜
Lower than something.
Ex 1: The dog is sleeping below the table.
Ex 2: The ants are below the leaf.
Near 🔊
/nɪər/
📍
Close to something.
Ex 1: The ball is near the box.
Ex 2: The cat is near the window.
In front of 🔊
/ɪn frʌnt əv/
🚗
Standing before something.
Ex 1: The car is in front of the house.
Ex 2: The teacher is in front of the board.
Taller 🔊
/ˈtɔː.lər/
🦒
More tall than another.
Ex 1: The giraffe is taller than the horse.
Ex 2: My dad is taller than me.
Smaller 🔊
/ˈsmɔː.lər/
🐁
More small than another.
Ex 1: A mouse is smaller than a cat.
Ex 2: Your bag is smaller than mine.
Faster 🔊
/ˈfɑː.stər/
🐆
More fast than another.
Ex 1: A cheetah is faster than a dog.
Ex 2: He is running faster than you.
